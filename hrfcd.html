The Hour of Code

On November 3rd, we visited Baku Secondary School No. 270 to host the Hour of Code.
There were 20 students, all of whom were seventh graders. Firstly, we showed students a presentation,
and Rafig Isayev talked about ADA University. During this event, we were teaching students
the basics of computer science. Sadig Hasanli explained the basics of that course
so it would be easier for students to start solving these tasks by themselves.
After an explanation, students solved these problems in code.org,
and Novruz Mirishli and Jamal Izabakarov helped them solve them correctly.
